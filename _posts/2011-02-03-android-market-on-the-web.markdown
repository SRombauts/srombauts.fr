The [Android Market](https://market.android.com/apps/) is now fully on the Web.
It allows you to browse, to buy, but also to push applications directly on your mobile!

![Android Market on the Web](/assets/images/android-web-market.png)

From a developer perspective, this means that:

- [when publishing a new version of your application](https://market.android.com/publish/) you need to provide high quality assets for this web showcase
- when writing about applications, you should now provide direct market links in addition of the well known QR-code.

Take a look at the market to see how publishers are using this new media to advertise their applications !

Edit: give a look at [Publishing on Android Market](http://developer.android.com/guide/publishing/publishing.html) on the Android Developer website.
